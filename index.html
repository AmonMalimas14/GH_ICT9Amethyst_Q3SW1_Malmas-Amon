The avengers
Summary: Well known unique heroes met one another and had no idea what to do, they had their own origin on how they gotten their abilities. Now with a new threat, they must defend earth from an alien like invasion or the eart shall face the consequences
Tony Stark: He is the man who built a armor machine that can do things that a human cannot do. It is equipped with steel which can be bullet proof, and it could shoot bullets or rockets. This machine can help Tony Stark defend himself from any threats.
Captain America: He was a soldier before but later crashed into the ice in the middle of no where. He was luckily found and he was the first person who was put in the avengers. He only has a shield but he has good combat skills which makes him deadly. He can also throw his shield as a projectile to hit enemies from a far.
